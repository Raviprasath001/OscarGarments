The Legacy and the Impact

We are a professionally managed clothing company at Tirupur over the past two decades, which has achieved significant growth in the manufacture and export of superior knitwear and woven garments.


OSCAR GARMENTS answers the market need for fine Jersey knitwear, with a 100% Export Oriented Garment Manufacturing Unit in a vertically integrated facility. With the initiative to endeavour by the visionaries and the commitment in efforts by the skilled workforce, our presence has gone places.

EVOLVING GLOBALLY

Our sewing factory at Bangladesh helps cater to the requirement of fashion conscious people all over the world in the realm of Jersey and woven garments.

VISION

To grow continually as a producer of men, women and kids wear, laying emphasis on Quality, Integrity and Time,
ensuing customer delight.

MISSION

To review quality objectives from time to time and develop high profile garments by perpetual research on fabrics,
accessories and fashion trends.

INFRASTRUCTURE

Increasing productivity & profitability

Our contemporary machinery comprises of in-house Knitting, Dyeing, Finishing, Printing, Embroidery and Washing. We have a stringent Quality Management System in place, ultra-tech equipments and inspired human resources to produce exclusive and diverse clothing options to the discerning consumer.

SUSTAINABILITY

Care for the eco-system

At OSCAR GARMENTS, we believe in the cause to save our environment by adhering to Government, local bodies and standards of international buyers, keeping in mind systematic clean room practices in the industry.

KNITTING

Mastering the precision

We produce an assorted choice of knitting in various designs and textures with advanced Mayer & Cie machines from Germany & Pailung circular knitting machines from Taiwan and highest quality yarns.

PRINTING & EMBROIDERY

Inheriting the rainbow

Plastisol, Glitter, Pigment and Foil Printing are some of the variants of screen printing. Azo-free dyes and chemicals are used and the customized prints in eye-catching colours leave a durable impression on the finished garments. The Printing Unit is equipped appropriately to produce 26,000 pieces per day and up to 20 colors in one design with automatic M&R machines. Size is not a restriction as we can cover print areas practically bigger than garments, the maximum size being 80 cm x 110 cm! We have TAJIMA & BARUDAN with capability to handle sequence embroidery and a capacity close to half million stitches per day.

DYEING

Clean and Nature friendly

Technologically always ahead in sustainable manufacturing, we have installed earth-positive machines like German THIES Soft flow Dyeing machines, Swiss-made SANTEX Padding & Drying machine, DATACOLOR Lab Equipment & Compacting machines from USA, German Bruckner Stenter and Italian Ferraro Brushing and Raising machines.Our Effluent Treatment Plant is in conformance with the Government, with RO Unit, Multiple Evaporators and Zero Discharge Unit by adapting innovative strategies from time to time.

SEWING

Deft & agile workmanship

With the help of Lectra CAD expertise, the fabric undergoes immaculate cutting processes in multifarious styles at the cutting department. At our four closely located sewing units, the fusion of world class sewing machines and a hard working team ensures a capacity of 50,000 units a day. Every piece of garment satisfies all the parameters.

WASHING

Comprehensive & Functional

Our In-house washing handles fabric panels & garments with a variety of washes.

TESTIMONIALS

Our many prestigious international clienteles like Mayoral, Desigual, Mango, US Polo, Lee, Wrangler and Levis will vouch for the superior quality of our garments, always exceeding their expectations, time and again.

ACCREDITATIONS

Quality Certifications like WRAP, SA8000 and SEDEX and the Government of India recognition as a Two Star Export Unit assure the best sustainability and unparalleled quality of our products.
